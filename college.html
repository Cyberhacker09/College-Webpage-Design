About Us

Company Profile and Brand: EThames College, Hyderabad is a leading business school focused on transforming and improving management education. EThames is rated among top 10 best colleges for undergraduate studies in India and it's ranked 7 in South India in the Times BBA Ranking Survey. EThames is now among top 10 most promising BBA colleges in India by Higher Education Review 2022. Through its BBA, BCA & BCOM programmes with new age specialisations, EThames aims to make a significant influence on society by developing agile leaders for the society. We nurture mindsets that empower our students to question the current quo and take the necessary steps. We train our students to succeed in the corporate world by using cutting-edge technology and the most up-to-date global management tools and methodologies.. We believe that the root of all achievement is able management. Hence, to inculcate management skills in our students we have different clubs where a lot of interaction takes place. We have Finance club, Marketing club, HR club, Literary club and Placement club. Activities in these clubs ensure students learn different managerial skills. These clubs also ensure building resilient connections with the industries not just for modernising the course curriculum as per industry requirement but also for outstanding placements. EThames encourages entrepreneurship and has launched its own incubator centre, which provides funding to promising business ideas and startups.
